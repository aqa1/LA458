The map below shows the the population of people who are 50 years of age or older.


As shown below it also compares the vegetation and bodies of water in Ames, IA in comparison to population of 50+ years. It is interesting to see that there is a good amount of elders in both the rural area and also towards the center of the city. This means that there is probably a good amount of elder people living on farm land which explains the high number of people living close to vegetation.
