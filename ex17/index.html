Learning to add Custom styled Mapbox Basemaps

Alejandra Quintana
April 30th, 2019


For “Alejandra’s MapBox” layer, I focused on understanding how to change different layers. This layer is
focused on showing the main roads and water. Layers I changed include settlement subdivision, road, water line, water,
waterway, national park, land structure polygon, and deleted many labels to clean up the map. I mostly focused on understanding
how to change color and ling weights with zoom ranges.



For layer “MapBox #2” I wanted to focus more on the city buildings and national parks. The major roads
are white to show to show them but as you zoom in you will see some changes. In this map I changed the national parks, waterway,
road, deleted label layers, and background.
