About Polk County Iowa:
• It is Iowa's most populated county at 481,830 people as of 2017. This county hosts about 14% of Iowa's residents and stores the capital city, Des Moines.
• The county is home to the following cities; Alleman, Altoona, Ankeny, Bondurant, Carlisle, Clive, Des Moines, Elkhart, Grimes, Granger, Johnston, Mitchelville, Norwalk, Pleasant Hill, Polk City, Runnells, Sheldahl, Urbandale, West Des Moines, and Windsor Heights.

County Attractions:
• Des Moines has many attraction for its residents and near by cities such as malls, parks, lakes, etc.
• 11.7% of the population live below the poverty line, a number that is lower than the national average of 13.4%.
• The median household income is $65,995.
• The median property value in Polk County is $180,000.

• If a family's total income is less than the family's threshold than that family and every individual in it is considered to be living in poverty.
