Sparkling Beach Information

Located in the Indian ocean, south of India, the Maldives had enjoyable tropical weather throughout the entire year (between 84 to 90 degrees fahrenheit). The beach Vaadhoo on the Maldives island, is famously known as the "sea of starts" or sparkling beach of the Maldives. The sparkling stars in the beach comes from bioluminescence, a process when plankton come in contact with oxygen and unleashes a chemical reaction that is caused from an enzyme names luciferase.
